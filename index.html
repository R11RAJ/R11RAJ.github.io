About Me

My name is Raj Prajapati (So easy to guess right!!),I am a Computer science student currently pursuing Master of Technology program in Machine Learning from Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology, Gandhinagar. I love coding and figuring out solutions for complex problems. I have completed my high school from Kendriya Vidyalaya Silvassa and bachelors in engineering from Parul University Vadodara in Computer Science and Engineering with a CPI of 9.02 out of 10.

I have been into this field since my 11th grade i.e.from 2014. I am a quick learner and always want to explore new programming languages and frameworks. I was an Erasmus student in 2017 and went to Wroclaw University of Science and Technology for a fully funded semester exchange program known as Erasmus+ Program. So far I have travelled across 9 countries in Europe and visited a lot of famous places like Eiffel tower , Camp Nou Stadium in barcelona , Atomium centre brussels etc. Yes I am a traveller and adventure lover.

Currently I am doing my Masters Thesis work and also work as a Teaching Assistant @DAIICT Gandhinagar.

My Areas of Interests are Machine Learning , Data Science , Natural Language Processing and Human Computer Interaction.
